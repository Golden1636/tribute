THE BIOGRAPHY OF

LATE MAZI NATHANIEL OGBURIE

MADUKUBA AMADI

(A.K.A AMADI AMIKE)

BACKGROUND

Late Mazi Nathaniel O M Amadi was born into the family of Late Mazi Forester Nze and Late Roseline Oyiridiye Nnani Ogburi Amadi of Umuwanu Amike Orlu Local Government Area of Imo State on 7th May, 1930. He had a very humble but adventurous and eventful beginning and upbringing. He had a very strong inclination and love for the things of God. This drive motivated him to be a mass servant at St peter's Catholic Church Amike from 1947-1950.

EDUCATION

He started his primary school education at St. Peter's Primary School Amike in 1938 and successfully completed his primary school education in 1946 where he obtained his First School Leaving Standard Six Certificate. It was during this period, he lost his father at very tender age. This did not in any way discourage him from forging ahead being the only son of the family, he rose up to the challenge by sponsoring his secondary school education with the support of his mother. He proceeded to Colony School of Commerce Ebute Metta Lagos State in 1950 and successfully graduated with flying colours. Thereafter, he pressed forward to upgrade his educational pursuits by taking up correspondence courses in Secretarial Studies and business training at Business Training Institute, Speakers House 39 Deansgate Mal England, and M3 and obtained a Diploma in Business and Secretarial studies with upper credit on August 23rd 1967, hence he was a seasoned Administrator.

WORKING CAREER AND ACHIEVEMENTS.

He worked in various organizations, holding managerial positions with impeccable and enviable track-record to his credit. Below is a brief extract of some key working experience amongst a host of others: Bible Society of Nigeria Appa Lagos-Stenographer (1971-1974). He thereafter worked at Trans Atlantic Nigeria Ltd from 1975-1982, a company he co-founded with his British Counterpart, late Mr. colman Edward. The Company grew in leaps and bounds. He single handedly pioneered the growth and progress of the company from its cradle until, it became a household name in the country then. Thereafter, he secured another managerial appointment with FiNA White House Group Hostels Kaduna in 1987 as the General/Admin Manager, a position he held until 1996. At this juncture, he resigned to float his own Company, Amadia and Sons Integrated Investment Limited, he was the chairman and Chief Executive Officer, a position he held until his demise.

SOCIAL SERVICES/RELIGIOUS LIFE

He was a very committed and devoted Christian who grew up in the lord as a member of St. peter's Catholic Amike in Group one men. He was a vibrant and sociable person as well and his love for sociaL activities, made him to be a member of many social groups and these include Omeokachie Social Club of Nigeria, Igwenmadu Social Club of Nigeria. He was also a member of the Catholic Charismatic Renewal Movement, St Joseph Catholic Church Kaduna...

LIFE STYLE

Late Mazi Nathaniel Amadi lived a very simple and humble life. He was a man who stood out for justice, fairness and equity. He was a peace loving man who toed the paths of peace, a legacy he bequeathed to his children. He got married to Late Mrs. Eucharia Amadi (Nee Nneoma) in 1969. Their union was blessed with six children. Sometimes we wonder why things happen to people with good heart. The Bible says in Isaiah 57:1 "that God takes, righteous people die and no one cares, loyal people are taken away and no one understands that God takes them away to spare them from the evil to come" indeed there is a time to be born and the time to die. we will surely miss you Daddy, however, we thank God for his life of goodness and ultimately for granting him eternal rest because he embraced and gave his life to Christ while on earth.
Good bye Daddy, until we meet to part no more.
